Laura Dylchenko
A quiet corner of Sochi overgrown with ivy is an endangered monument of architecture of federal significance - the sanatorium named after Ordzhonikidze in the city of Sochi. nobody is engaged in restoration of the monument, cracks go through the buildings, stucco molding falls off, everything is overgrown with ivy. The film-tale “Old Man-Hottabych” and other Soviet films were shot here.
